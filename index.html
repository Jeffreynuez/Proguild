Mission & Core Values

Dependable service, delivered with integrity

Proguild exists to keep commercial properties safe, functional, and welcoming. We combine skilled trades, modern coordination, and a genuine sense of responsibility to the people and communities our buildings serve.

Whether it's a midnight emergency or a planned preventive program, we show up prepared, communicate clearly, and finish the job right — because the trust of the people we work for is the only foundation worth building on.
